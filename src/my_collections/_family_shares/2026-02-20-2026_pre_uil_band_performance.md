I actually don't fully understand the full context of this performance. I think this is the result of Evan being in honors band. I believe this is leading up to another performance and the very cool [AISD Performing Arts Center](https://www.austinisd.org/arts/pac). I think this was the lead up event, and it included a very brief 15 minute performance.

After the performance, they went into another room and did a sight reading exercise. This was very cool. There was some master judge type dude who was a band director for around 25 years. Evan's band director I think had been given something like 3 minutes to look at a sheet of music. The band was then given the music for the first time, and the director then had 7 minutes to prepare the students to learn and perform the piece. They then did a quick performance of the music they'd just been handed.

It was a very cool experience. Was really cool to see how the director crammed as much instruction as possible into 7 minutes. It was also impressive how well the students did in playing the music. There were several issues that even my untrained ear could perceive. But it was overall so good and so impressive, especially when considering that most of these kids have been playing instruments for around 2.5 years at this point.
