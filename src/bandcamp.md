### Some ideas for why Evan has enjoyed and benefited from band camp

- It allowed him to meet new friends and to deepen relationships with existing ones
- It gave him a sense of independence and taught him responsibility. For instance, buying his own beverages with Apple Pay and having to remember to charge his phone.
- It exposed him to new types of music. For instance, he gained a deeper appreciation of salsa music.
- It showed him that intense focus and practice over a short period of time can lead to a surprising degree of improvement over the course of just a few days.
- It taught him a sense of camaraderie and team work in learning to play music together with people he'd only just met.
